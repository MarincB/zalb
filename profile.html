I got my first real experiences working for a startup Zemanta. It was a well-establish startup with a working product for content advertising, with offices in San Francisco and New York and Ljubljana where I worked. I learned a lot about optimizing content and user experience through interaction and of course working with developers.

After a good year and a half, I decided to join a digital agency Gigodesign. It was quite a different experience going form the cozy startup to a much more fast-paced agency, working on more projects at a time and fast-changing clients, requests...

I think it was a good decision to jump the boat because it made me a way better designer more adaptive, and I experienced a more fast-paced way of designing. I got the opportunity to work with some of the biggest clients from our county as well as clients from all over the world. I was also really involved in optimizing our design/development process. I believe that every project gives you new knowledge and experiences that make you a better designer and makes you take better decisions in the future. Still working at Gigodesign but I'm ready to make a new step forward...

On that note, I always looking for new challenges and experiences!

So if you would like to talk about my projects, experiences, design systems or something else drop me a line.
